Roadcase is the all-in-one tour management platform that replaces your scattered spreadsheets, email threads, and group chats with a single purpose-built tool. Whether you are a band leader planning a national run, a musician tracking your schedule, or a crew member managing day-of logistics, Roadcase keeps your entire operation organized.

## Who is Roadcase for?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Administrators" icon="crown">
    Band leaders, managers, and booking agents who need full control over shows, deals, settlements, and team management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Members" icon="guitar">
    Musicians and performers who need to view schedules, manage availability, request guest list spots, and track setlists.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crew" icon="wrench">
    Tour managers, sound engineers, lighting techs, merch sellers, and other crew who manage day-of logistics and operations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What can you do with Roadcase?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Plan your tour" icon="calendar" href="/tour-planning/shows">
    Create shows, manage venues, and build out your tour calendar with all event types in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage show operations" icon="sliders" href="/show-operations/show-schedule">
    Handle show schedules, deals, settlements, guest lists, advances, stage plots, and riders.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track your music" icon="music" href="/music/songs">
    Build a song library, create setlists, and manage what you play at every show.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stay productive" icon="list-check" href="/productivity/todos">
    Assign todos, manage projects, organize documents, and track expenses across your team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Promote your artist" icon="bullhorn" href="/marketing/epk">
    Create a public Electronic Press Kit with your bio, photos, press quotes, and social links.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Take it on the road" icon="mobile" href="/account/mobile-app">
    Access everything from the Roadcase iOS and Android apps while you are on tour.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
