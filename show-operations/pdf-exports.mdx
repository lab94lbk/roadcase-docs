Roadcase can generate PDF documents for the key artifacts of every show. Whether you need a settlement receipt for the promoter, a guest list for the box office, a day sheet for the band, or a setlist for the stage, you can export a clean, branded PDF in one click.

## What you can export

| Document | Where to export | What it includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Settlement** | Show > Settlement tab > **Export PDF** | Deal terms, revenue breakdown, expenses, payout calculation, artist logo |
| **Guest list** | Show > Guest list tab > **Export PDF** | Approved guests with names, quantities, pass types, and total count |
| **Day sheet** | Show > Schedule tab > **Export PDF** | Full day-of timeline with venue details, contacts, and schedule items |
| **Setlist** | Show > Setlist tab > **Export PDF** | Song list with set order and any notes |

## Branding

Every exported PDF automatically includes your artist logo if one has been uploaded in **Settings > Profile**. This gives your documents a professional, branded appearance when shared with venues, promoters, and business contacts.

<Tip>
  Upload a high-resolution logo in your artist settings to ensure your PDFs look crisp when printed. PNG or SVG formats work best.
</Tip>

## Common uses

PDF exports are designed for the real-world moments when you need a physical or shareable document:

- **Settlements** — Hand a printed settlement to the promoter at the end of the night, or email it to your accountant
- **Guest lists** — Print the guest list and leave it at the box office or with the door person
- **Day sheets** — Distribute to the band and crew so everyone knows the day's timeline
- **Setlists** — Tape to the stage floor, hand to the sound engineer, or keep for your records

## Generating a PDF

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the show">
    Open the show from the calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the relevant tab">
    Open the Settlement, Guest list, Schedule, or Setlist tab depending on which document you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export PDF">
    Click the **Export PDF** button. The PDF is generated and downloaded to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  PDF exports reflect the current state of the data at the time of export. If you update information after exporting, generate a new PDF to get the latest version.
</Note>
