Riders are documents that outline your technical and hospitality requirements for a show. Roadcase supports two types of riders, both with rich-text editing, built-in templates, and PDF export.

## Rider types

### Technical rider

Your tech rider specifies the production requirements for your performance:

- Front-of-house (FOH) sound requirements
- Monitor requirements
- Backline needs
- Lighting requirements
- Power requirements
- Stage dimensions and load-in details

### Hospitality rider

Your hospitality rider specifies food, beverage, and comfort requirements:

- Dressing room requirements
- Food and catering
- Beverages
- Towels, toiletries, and other amenities
- Parking and transportation

## Creating a rider

1. Navigate to **Riders** in the sidebar
2. Click **New rider**
3. Choose the rider type: **Tech** or **Hospitality**
4. Give it a name (e.g., "Standard tech rider" or "Festival hospitality rider")

### Using built-in templates

Roadcase provides starter templates for both rider types:

- **Standard tech rider template** — Pre-structured sections for FOH, monitors, backline, lighting, and power
- **Standard hospitality rider template** — Pre-structured sections for catering, beverages, dressing room, and amenities

Select a template when creating a new rider to start with a professional structure. Then customize the content for your specific needs.

### Rich-text editing

Rider content is authored with a rich-text editor that supports:

- Headings and subheadings
- Bold, italic, and underline formatting
- Bulleted and numbered lists
- Tables
- Links

## Using riders in advances

Riders are a core component of [show advances](/show-operations/advances). When you share an advance with a venue, your tech and hospitality riders are displayed as formatted documents.

Attach riders to [advance templates](/show-operations/advances) so they are automatically included every time you advance a show.

## Exporting riders

Click **Export PDF** to generate a printable rider document. The PDF includes:

- Rider content with all formatting preserved
- Artist logo (if uploaded)

The PDF is designed to be emailed to venues or printed for on-site reference.

## Multiple riders

Create multiple riders for different scenarios:

- **Club tech rider** — Standard requirements for club shows
- **Festival tech rider** — Adjusted requirements for festival stages (where backline may be shared)
- **Acoustic rider** — Simplified requirements for stripped-down performances
- **Green room rider** — Detailed hospitality requirements for larger shows

Attach the appropriate rider to each show's advance based on the venue and situation.
