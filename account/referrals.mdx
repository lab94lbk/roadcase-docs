Roadcase's referral program rewards you for spreading the word. When someone you refer subscribes to Roadcase, both of you earn credits toward your subscription.

## How it works

1. **Get your referral code** — Find your unique referral code in **Settings > Referrals**
2. **Share it** — Give your referral code to other artists, managers, and musicians
3. **They sign up** — The referred person enters your code when they create their account
4. **You both earn credits** — Once the referred person subscribes, both of you receive credits

## Referral credits

Credits are applied toward your subscription, reducing what you pay on your next billing cycle. The exact credit amounts are shown on your referral settings page.

## Tracking referrals

Your referral settings page shows:

- Your unique referral code
- How many people have used your code
- Credits earned
- Credit history

## Sharing your code

Share your referral code however works best:

- In person at shows and industry events
- In text messages or group chats
- On social media
- In email to friends and colleagues

## Applying a referral code

If someone shared a referral code with you:

1. Enter it during account creation, or
2. Navigate to **Settings > Referrals** and enter it there

The code must be applied before or shortly after subscribing for credits to be awarded to both parties.
