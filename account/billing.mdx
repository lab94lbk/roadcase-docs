Roadcase billing is managed through Stripe. You can view your current plan, upgrade or downgrade, and manage your payment method from the billing settings.

## Viewing your plan

Navigate to **Settings > Billing** to see:

- Your current plan (Basic or Pro)
- Billing cycle and next payment date
- Payment method on file

## Subscribing

After your [14-day free trial](/plans-and-pricing), choose the plan that fits your needs:

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | $10/month | Core features — calendar, shows, team, guest lists, todos, songs, setlists, documents, expenses |
| **Pro** | $40/month | Everything in Basic + deals, settlements, advances, stage plots, riders, projects, EPK, organizations |

See [Plans & pricing](/plans-and-pricing) for a full feature comparison.

## Upgrading

To upgrade from Basic to Pro:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade to Pro**
3. Confirm your payment method
4. Pro features are unlocked immediately

You are charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your current billing cycle.

## Downgrading

To downgrade from Pro to Basic:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change plan**
3. Select Basic
4. Confirm

The downgrade takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You retain Pro access until then.

When your plan switches to Basic:

- Pro-only features become read-only
- Your existing Pro data (deals, settlements, advances, stage plots, riders, projects, EPK) is preserved
- You cannot create new Pro content until you upgrade again

## Payment method

Update your credit card or payment method from the billing settings page. Roadcase uses Stripe for secure payment processing — your card details are never stored on Roadcase servers.

## Grace period

If your subscription lapses (payment fails, card expires, etc.), there is a **7-day grace period** during which your account continues to function normally. This gives you time to update your payment method without disruption.

After the grace period, your account reverts to a limited state until payment is resolved.

## Invoices

Invoices are available in the billing section. Each billing cycle generates an invoice that you can view or download for your records.
