The Roadcase mobile app gives you access to your touring operations from anywhere. Available for both iOS and Android, the app is designed for on-the-road use when you need quick access to your schedule, show details, and team information.

## Getting started

1. Download the Roadcase app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com) (iOS) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com) (Android)
2. Open the app and sign in with your email
3. You will receive a magic link — tap it to authenticate
4. Your artists and data appear automatically

## What you can do on mobile

The mobile app includes the features you need most while on the road:

### Calendar & events

- View your full calendar with all event types
- See event details including show schedules, venues, and notes
- Filter by artist

### Show details

- View show information with lazy-loaded tabs for:
  - Guest list
  - Deal details
  - Settlement information
  - Advance details
  - Attendance

### Guest lists

- Submit guest list requests
- View guest list status (pending, approved, denied)
- Administrators can approve or deny requests from mobile

### Todos

- View your assigned todos
- Mark todos complete
- See overdue tasks

### Expenses

- Submit expenses on the go
- Attach receipt photos directly from your camera
- View expense history

### Documents

- Browse your document library
- View and download documents
- Access event-specific documents

### Notifications

- Receive push notifications for important changes
- View your notification inbox
- Manage notification preferences

## Authentication

The mobile app uses token-based authentication. When you sign in, a secure API token is created for your device. The token tracks:

- Device name and type
- Last used timestamp
- Expiration

Your session remains active until you explicitly sign out or the token expires.

## Push notifications

Enable push notifications during setup to stay informed while away from your computer. The app supports all the same notification types as the web app.

See [Notifications](/account/notifications) for details on configuring what notifications you receive.

## Offline access

The mobile app requires an internet connection for most operations. Content that has been previously loaded may be viewable offline, but creating, editing, and syncing data requires connectivity.
