Roadcase integrates with Bandsintown so you can manage your shows in one place and keep your public-facing tour dates up to date. Import existing Bandsintown events into Roadcase, or sync your Roadcase shows out to Bandsintown — either way, you avoid double-entry and reduce the chance of mismatched dates.

## Importing from Bandsintown

If you already have shows listed on Bandsintown, you can pull them into Roadcase:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Bandsintown account">
    Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** and connect your Bandsintown artist profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review available events">
    Roadcase fetches your upcoming Bandsintown events and displays them for review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import events">
    Select the events you want to import. Roadcase creates show events on your calendar with the venue, date, and time pre-filled from Bandsintown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Imported events become full Roadcase shows, so you can add schedules, deals, advances, guest lists, and everything else.

## Syncing to Bandsintown

When you create or update shows in Roadcase, synced shows are pushed to your Bandsintown artist page. This means your fans always see accurate tour dates without you needing to update Bandsintown separately.

| What syncs | Details |
|---|---|
| **New shows** | Confirmed shows are pushed to Bandsintown |
| **Date and time changes** | Updated automatically |
| **Venue changes** | Updated automatically |
| **Cancellations** | Removed from Bandsintown |

<Note>
  Only show events sync to Bandsintown. Other event types (rehearsals, meetings, travel, etc.) are not published to your public Bandsintown profile.
</Note>

## Venue matching

When importing events, Roadcase attempts to match Bandsintown venues with venues already in your Roadcase account. If a match is found, the imported show is automatically linked to your existing venue record. If no match is found, Roadcase creates a new venue from the Bandsintown data.

## Managing the connection

You can manage your Bandsintown integration from **Settings > Integrations**:

- **Enable or disable sync** — Pause syncing without disconnecting
- **Disconnect** — Remove the Bandsintown connection entirely

<Tip>
  Connect Bandsintown early when setting up your artist in Roadcase. Importing your existing shows saves time compared to re-entering them manually.
</Tip>

## Why use both platforms

Roadcase is your internal tour management tool — it handles the operational side of touring that your team needs. Bandsintown is your public-facing event listing that fans use to discover and track your shows. The integration bridges the two so your internal planning and public listings stay in sync without extra work.
