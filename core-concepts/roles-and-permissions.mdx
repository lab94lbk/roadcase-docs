Roadcase uses a role-based access system. Every team member is assigned one of three roles when they join an artist. The role determines what they can view, create, edit, and manage.

## Administrator

Administrators have full, unrestricted access to everything within an artist. This role is designed for:

- **Band leaders** who make decisions about shows, finances, and team composition
- **Managers** who handle the business side of touring
- **Booking agents** who need to create and manage shows

Key administrator capabilities:
- Create, edit, and delete all event types
- Manage financial deal structures and run settlements
- Create and share show advances with venues
- Approve or deny guest list requests
- View all expenses from all team members
- Manage team membership and roles
- Configure billing and subscription
- Access admin-only documents and dashboard widgets

An artist must always have at least one administrator.

## Member

Members have read access to the calendar and shows, with the ability to manage their own personal items. This role is designed for:

- **Musicians and performers** in the band
- **Songwriters** and collaborators

Key member capabilities:
- View the full calendar and all event details
- Block dates to mark personal unavailability
- Request guest list spots for shows (subject to admin approval)
- Submit expenses and view their own expense history
- Manage songs and build setlists
- Complete assigned todos
- Upload documents

Members cannot see other members' block dates, view financial deal details (if restricted), or manage team settings.

## Crew

Crew members have access focused on logistics and day-of operations. This role is designed for:

- **Tour managers** coordinating logistics
- **Sound engineers and lighting techs** managing technical requirements
- **Merchandise sellers** tracking inventory
- **Drivers, photographers, and videographers**

Key crew capabilities:
- View the full calendar and all event details
- Submit expenses and view their own expense history
- Complete assigned todos
- Upload documents
- View stage plots and riders

Crew members have similar access to members but cannot manage songs or setlists.

## Organization roles

If your artist belongs to an [Organization](/core-concepts/organizations), there is an additional layer of organization-level roles:

- **Organization admin** — Can manage all artists within the organization
- **Organization member** — Can access specific artists they have been granted access to

Organization roles can override artist-level roles for specific contexts. See [Organizations](/core-concepts/organizations) for details.
