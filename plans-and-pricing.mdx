## Free trial

Every new account gets a 14-day free trial with full Pro access. No credit card is required to start. During the trial, you have access to every feature including deals, settlements, advances, stage plots, riders, projects, EPK, and organizations.

When your trial ends, you can choose to subscribe to either plan. If you choose Basic, Pro-only features become read-only — your existing data is preserved, but you cannot create new Pro content until you upgrade.

## Upgrading and downgrading

You can change your plan at any time from **Settings > Billing**. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

If your subscription lapses, there is a 7-day grace period during which your account continues to function normally. After the grace period, your account reverts to a limited state until you resubscribe.
